Executive Operations and Strategy

Elevating Business Growth through Thoughtful Strategy.

My Journey

When I joined F5's Office of the CTO in May 2022 and transitioned into my Chief of Staff role in 2024, I stepped into a position that seamlessly combined strategic vision, operational execution, and leadership. This role centered on aligning market priorities with organizational goals, crafting multi-year product roadmaps, and driving impactful narratives.

I developed high-profile pitch decks and whitepapers that articulated our vision while also delivering tailored presentations shown to over 500 customers, partners, and prospects worldwide, ensuring our strategies resonated with diverse audiences and advanced key business objectives.

  • Served as the bridge between vision and execution, aligning stakeholders across departments.
  • Enabled leadership teams to translate ambitious goals into measurable outcomes.

Turning Vision Into Action

Strategy isn’t just about setting goals; it’s about ensuring every team knows their role in achieving them. Partnering with the CTO and senior leaders, I led the alignment of Objectives and Key Results (OKRs) to ensure our priorities supported business growth and innovation.

  • Facilitated strategic planning sessions, aligning FY25 OKRs to drive business growth and clarity.
  • Built reporting frameworks and dashboards to provide real-time progress tracking.
  • Streamlined workflows to eliminate bottlenecks and improve team productivity.
