Creating Opportunities for All

Building community has always been central to my career. Whether mentoring students, fostering diversity in tech, or empowering underserved communities, I am passionate about creating opportunities for everyone to thrive. My work spans STEM outreach, mentoring programs, and initiatives that celebrate inclusion and amplify underrepresented voices.

F5 Appreciates Blackness (FAB)

As a core member of F5’s diversity and inclusion efforts through the F5 Appreciates Blackness (FAB) Employee Inclusion Group (EIG), I worked to create an environment that celebrates and empowers Black employees and communities.

Key Contributions

  • Organized community events and educational programs to celebrate Black culture and contributions in tech.
  • Mentored students in F5’s direct internship in partnership with Howard University, providing guidance on technical projects, professional development, and navigating careers in tech.
  • Partnered with local organizations to provide underprivileged students with access to computer science education and mentorship.
  • Hosted panel discussions and workshops on topics like career advancement, allyship, and building equitable workplaces.
  • Represented F5 at major career conferences such as AfroTech and SWE (Society of Women Engineers), recruiting top talent and promoting diversity in tech.

Impact

  • Helped implement a culture of inclusivity and representation at F5, promoting dialogue and understanding across teams.
  • Expanded outreach to local communities, introducing hundreds of students to opportunities in STEM.

Technology Access Foundation (TAF): STEM Outreach Volunteer & Alliance Partner

As a STEM Outreach Volunteer and Alliance Partner with TAF, I worked to bridge the gap in STEM education for underserved communities by creating hands-on learning opportunities.

Key Contributions

  • Delivered STEM workshops for K-12 students, introducing them to engineering, programming, and design concepts.
  • Mentored students to explore careers in STEM through real-world applications and activities.
  • Served as an Alliance Partner, connecting TAF with industry resources and support to enhance their impact.

Impact

  • Inspired hundreds of students to explore STEM fields by creating fun and accessible programs.
  • Strengthened community partnerships, broadening access to resources for underrepresented students.

Girls Who Code: Mentor and Resource for Students

My time with Girls Who Code extended beyond teaching—I became a mentor and resource for students, supporting their journeys into technology and building their confidence as future technologists.

Key Contributions

  • Guided students during and after the Summer Immersion Program, offering technical advice, career guidance, and encouragement.
  • Facilitated discussions on navigating the tech industry, building confidence, and overcoming challenges as women in STEM.
  • Hosted workshops on practical skills like resume building, interview prep, and leveraging coding projects for career growth.

Impact

  • Helped students develop the confidence and skills to pursue STEM degrees and careers.
  • Built a lasting network of support, connecting students to opportunities and role models in the tech industry.

Looking Ahead

I believe that building inclusive communities isn’t just about opening doors—it’s about creating a sense of belonging for everyone who walks through them. I’m excited to continue mentoring, supporting diversity initiatives, and fostering connections that empower the next generation of innovators.
