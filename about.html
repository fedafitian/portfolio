About Me

Discover who I am and what drives my passion for innovation, strategy, and community building.

Hi, I'm Feda (Fee-da).

With over a decade of experience spanning software engineering, strategy, and program management, I’ve had the privilege of working on projects that merge innovation with impact. My work revolves around solving complex challenges, driving business growth, and empowering communities.

Based in Seattle, I specialize in creating solutions that make a difference—whether it's through product delivery, leading innovation initiatives, or building inclusive communities. I also strongly believe in the power of education to change lives.

My Leadership Style

I lead with empathy and inclusivity because I truly believe the best ideas come from diverse perspectives. Trust, respect, and collaboration are the foundation of any strong team, and it is a strong value of mine to make sure everyone I work with feels heard and empowered to contribute. I’m all about striking the right balance between big-picture strategy and taking meaningful action—making sure that every project I lead delivers real value and leaves a positive, lasting impact.
