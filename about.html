Hi, I'm Feda.

With over a decade of experience spanning software engineering, strategy, and program management, I’ve had the privilege of working on projects that merge innovation with impact. My work revolves around solving complex challenges, driving business growth, and empowering communities.

Based in Seattle, I specialize in creating solutions that make a difference—whether it's through product delivery, leading innovation initiatives, or building inclusive communities. Outside of work, I love exploring cultures and meeting new people. I’ve traveled to 29 countries, each one teaching me something unique about its history, traditions, and way of life. Along the way, I’ve called six different U.S. states home and picked up three languages, which has definitely helped me see the world from different perspectives and connect with all kinds of people.
